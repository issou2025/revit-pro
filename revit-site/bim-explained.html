Understanding BIM

Discover how Building Information Modeling is revolutionizing the AEC industry

What is Building Information Modeling?

Building Information Modeling (BIM) is a digital representation of physical and functional characteristics of a facility. It serves as a shared knowledge resource for information about a facility, forming a reliable basis for decisions during its lifecycle from inception onward.

BIM goes beyond 3D modeling by incorporating:

  • 4D (Time) - Construction Scheduling
  • 5D (Cost) - Cost Estimation
  • 6D (Sustainability) - Energy Analysis
  • 7D (Facility Management) - Operation & Maintenance

Benefits of BIM

Improved Coordination

Detect clashes early, reduce errors, and improve team collaboration throughout the project lifecycle.

Cost Control

Better cost estimation, reduced waste, and improved project efficiency through accurate quantity takeoffs.

Enhanced Visualization

Better understanding of the project through realistic 3D visualization and virtual walkthroughs.
